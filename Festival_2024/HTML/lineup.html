Line Up

Headliner: The Rockers

The Rockers are known for their electrifying performances and chart-topping hits. Get ready to rock out to their latest and greatest songs!

Special Guest: DJ Spinmaster

DJ Spinmaster will keep the energy high with his incredible mixes and beats. Don't miss out on his set that will keep you dancing all night long.

Band: The Jazz Ensemble

The Jazz Ensemble will bring smooth and soulful jazz to the stage, perfect for a relaxing and enjoyable experience.

Singer: Melody Waves

Melody Waves, with her captivating voice, will mesmerize the audience with her soulful ballads and upbeat tracks.

Comedy Act: Laughter Therapy

Laughter Therapy will have you in stitches with their hilarious comedy routines. A perfect way to lighten up the evening.

Dance Group: The Hip Hop Crew

The Hip Hop Crew will amaze you with their stunning dance moves and choreography. An act that's not to be missed!

Acoustic Performance: Strings Attached

Strings Attached will provide an intimate acoustic performance that showcases their musical talent and harmony.

Opening Act: Rising Star

Rising Star, a local favorite, will kick off the event with a powerful performance that sets the tone for an unforgettable night.
