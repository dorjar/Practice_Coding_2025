FAQ

What time does the event start?

The event starts at 10:00 AM on June 20th, 2024. Gates will open at 9:00 AM for early arrivals.

Where is the event located?

The event will take place at the Downtown Event Park, located at 123 Festival Street, Cityname.

Is parking available at the event?

Yes, there is ample parking available at the event venue. Parking passes can be purchased in advance or on the day of the event.

Are there any age restrictions for the event?

The event is open to all ages. However, certain areas like the Dance Arena may have age restrictions for safety reasons. Please check the specific stage information for details.

Can I bring outside food and drinks?

Outside food and drinks are not allowed. There will be a variety of food and beverage vendors inside the event offering a wide range of options.

What should I bring to the event?

We recommend bringing a valid ID, your ticket, comfortable clothing, sunscreen, and a hat. Please do not bring any prohibited items like weapons, drugs, or outside food and drinks.

Will there be ATMs available on-site?

Yes, ATMs will be available throughout the event venue for your convenience.

How can I contact the event organizers?

You can contact the event organizers through the Contact Us page on our website. Fill out the form, and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
